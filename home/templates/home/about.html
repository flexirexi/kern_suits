Our Master Tailors

Fritz is one of our two exclusive master tailors. He completed his training and earned his Master Craftsman qualification at the Chamber of Crafts in Stuttgart, one of Germany's most respected institutions for bespoke tailoring. His meticulous attention to detail and clear understanding of fit have shaped countless suits that our customers value for their precision and comfort.

Alberto bring the spirit of Italian Sartoria to our workshop. He learned the art of tailoring at the Scuola di Sartoria in Naples, a city renowned worldwide for its fine handmade suits. His sense for fabric and proportion adds a distinctive character to our collections, blending classic Italian elegance with contemporary design.
